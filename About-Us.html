The Vision Behind The Threads

Meet Raven, the soul behind Emotional Erased

Raven

Founder & Creative Director

"Clothing is the most intimate form of self-expression. Each piece I create is a chapter in someone's emotional journey."

With a background in both fine arts and psychology, Raven founded Emotional Erased in 2023 to bridge the gap between fashion and emotional expression. Every design starts as an emotion and transforms into wearable art.
